CNFTease - A social platform for Cardano

CNFTease aims to provide many capabilities to empower users to develop, maintain and interact with friends and followers.

The number one goal of CNFTease is to build a holistic user experience to finding, tracking and sharing your favorite CNFTs and projects.

Discord, twitter, telegram and many other tools are being patched together to provide social interactions and information.

As a project owner it can be difficult to keep up with a project's community.

As a user finding projects, getting updates, and interacting with a community can be a nightmare of noise.

CNFTease we will provide the right tools for the right people to build a platform for everyone.

CNFTease user personas

  1. Project managers
  2. Developers and designers
  3. Influencers
  4. CNFT Community members

Our minimum viable product (MVP)

  1. User and Project profile registration
  2. Event subscription system
  3. Notification and announcements
